Welcome to Islamabad

Experience the perfect blend of natural beauty and modern elegance in Pakistan’s serene capital. From the majestic Margalla Hills to the peaceful Faisal Mosque, Islamabad offers a refreshing journey through nature, culture, and innovation.

Plan your visit

Everything you need to know before visiting Islamabad

Best Time to Visit

March to May and September to November are ideal — the weather is pleasant, flowers bloom, and the Margalla Hills look stunning.

Culture & Etiquette

Islamabad is polite and conservative — dress modestly, especially at religious sites. Greet locals with a smile or a respectful “Assalam-o-Alaikum.”

Transport

Local options include Careem, InDrive, and Metro Bus, which are safe and affordable. Taxis are available, but always confirm the fare beforehand.

Food

Try Monal Restaurant for panoramic dining, Savour Foods for local pulao, and Street 1 Café for modern flavors. Islamabad’s food scene mixes desi tradition with continental taste.
